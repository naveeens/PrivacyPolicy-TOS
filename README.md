## üöÄ Quick Setup

### 1. Enable GitHub Pages

1. Go to your repository settings on GitHub
2. Navigate to "Pages" in the left sidebar
3. Under "Source", select the branch you want to deploy (usually `main` or `master`)
4. Select "/ (root)" as the folder
5. Click "Save"
6. Your site will be published at `https://naveeens.github.io/PrivacyPolicy-TOS/`

### 2. Customize the Content

You need to update the placeholder content with your actual information:

#### Privacy Policy (`privacy-policy.html`)
- Replace `[Your Email Address]` with your actual email
- Replace `[Your Address]` with your actual address (if required)
- Update any specific data collection practices based on your apps
- Adjust the third-party services section based on what you actually use

#### Terms of Service (`terms-of-service.html`)
- Replace `[Your Email Address]` with your actual email
- Replace `[Your Address]` with your actual address (if required)
- Update the governing law section with your jurisdiction
- Adjust any specific terms based on your app's functionality

### 3. Using in Google Play Console

When submitting or updating your Android app:

1. In the Google Play Console, go to your app
2. Navigate to "Store presence" ‚Üí "Store listing"
3. Under "Privacy Policy", enter:
   - Privacy Policy URL: `https://naveeens.github.io/PrivacyPolicy-TOS/privacy-policy.html`
4. For Terms of Service (if required), use:
   - Terms URL: `https://naveeens.github.io/PrivacyPolicy-TOS/terms-of-service.html`
